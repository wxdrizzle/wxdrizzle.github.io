I am joining Ant Group as an applied scientist in medical AI, working with Dr. Le Lu. I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Washington in 2026, co-advised by Prof. Linda Shapiro and Prof. Chun Yuan. Before my Ph.D., I worked as a research assistant in the School of Data Science at Fudan University, supervised by Prof. Xiahai Zhuang. I obtained my Bachelor’s degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering at Fudan University in 2020, advised by Prof. Jinhua Yu.

My research interests include medical image analysis and machine learning. I focus on developing novel multimodal models to advance disease prevention, diagnosis, and prognosis.
